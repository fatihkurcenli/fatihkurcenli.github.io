Information We Collect and Use

The apps may collect limited technical and usage information needed to operate, maintain, secure, analyze, and improve the apps. This may include device information, app interactions, diagnostics, crash reports, app performance data, device or other identifiers, IP address, operating system version, language, and approximate region derived from network information.

Some app features use content that you choose to provide. AR Drawing may use your camera and selected images to help you trace or sketch. Easy PDF Scanner may use your camera, selected images, and document files to create PDFs. This content is used to provide the feature you request and is primarily processed on your device unless you choose to share, export, or otherwise transmit it.

The apps do not require you to create an account, and they do not intentionally collect precise GPS location.

Permissions

  • Camera access may be requested for AR tracing, drawing guidance, document scanning, or image capture.
  • Photos, media, or file access may be requested when you choose an image, save artwork, import content, or create PDF documents.
  • Network access may be used for ads, analytics, crash reporting, app updates, and Google Play related services.

Third-Party Services

The apps may use third-party services that process data under their own privacy policies. These services help with distribution, advertising, analytics, diagnostics, crash reporting, and app quality.

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Advertising and Analytics

Some apps may display ads through Google AdMob. Advertising and analytics services may use device identifiers, app activity, diagnostics, and related signals to deliver ads, measure performance, detect issues, and improve reliability.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by trusted service providers used by the apps, disclosed when required by law, or used when necessary to protect users, prevent abuse, respond to legal requests, or maintain app security and reliability.

Retention and Deletion

Most app content is stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share it. You can delete local content by using app controls where available, clearing app data, or uninstalling the app. For privacy questions or deletion requests related to data we can access, contact us at autumnsunapp@gmail.com.

Children

The apps are intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed through the apps. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to keep the apps reliable and protected.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.
